About

Travel Explorer is a web application built with Flask that helps users discover and explore
amazing travel destinations around the world. Whether you're looking for popular tourist spots or
hidden gems, Travel Explorer has got you covered.

With features like destination exploration, search functionality, and personalized travel suggestions,
Travel Explorer makes trip planning easy and enjoyable. Start your travel journey with us
and explore the world like never before!
